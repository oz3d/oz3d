AUSTRALIAN VIEWMASTER REELS VARIATIONS PAGE - CONTRIBUTIONS WELCOMED

There seem to be very few variations in Australian reels and some of them follow similar varions in Belgian or US reels of the same number. This was part of a more ambitions 1995 variations page which included worlwide variations hence the now superfluous references to other editions in the world.
